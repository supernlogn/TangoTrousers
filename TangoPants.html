Behind the Table…

These pants are meant mainly for tango training and they give a loose feeling while dancing. They can be used as clothes of course outside tango giving a nice mediterranean (or havana) vibe. The makers of these pants are themselves Mediterranean and really appreciate and follow this style. They usually combine them with other clothes everyday or more formal to give a nice look depending on the occasion. These types of pants are already tested in dancing and especially by tango dancers in Thessaloniki who wear them in events. So, behind the table stands Ioannis, a guy who wears them after discussion with friends, thought of sharing his mother's creations and ideas to the wonderful milonga La Luna and no longer only to his hometown.
